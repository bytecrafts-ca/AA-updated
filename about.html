FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does a full detail take?

Most details take 3–6 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

PPF vs. ceramic—what's right for me?

PPF physically stops chips and scratches. Ceramic boosts gloss and makes cleaning easier. Many clients choose both.

What's included with ceramic coating?

Prep wash, decontamination, paint correction level as needed, and certified ceramic application with cure time.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes—our ceramic coatings carry manufacturer-backed warranties when applied by our certified technicians.

How do I maintain results?

We provide aftercare instructions and maintenance washes to keep protection performing for years.
