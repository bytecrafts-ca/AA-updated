Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

A&A AutoMods respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

When you contact us through our website form, we collect your name, email, phone number, and message content. We may also collect information you provide when scheduling appointments or requesting quotes.

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide quotes, schedule appointments, and deliver the automotive services you request. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
